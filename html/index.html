Our idea

Why are we creating the website?

We lived in a patriotic society for centuries, where the man's role was to provide for the family, while the woman's cared for the house and the children.But during the years of development and modernization of society, there have been several new opportunities and changes. Many women now have access to education and the opportunity to participate in working life. Still, there are more women in different parts of the world who do not have the same opportunities. They are not given the opportunity to participate in working life or develop further.

Who can use this website.

This idea focuses on women who have good business ideas, but who do not have enough resources to complete or further develop it. Our goal is to connect these women with investors from around the world. This will give underprivileged women a good opportunity to run their own business and be independent.

Impact of the idea on the future:

This solution will not only be useful for the ideator, but also for the local community and society. For example, they will be able to inspire other women to be independent and go in the same direction. further, if their idea were to get an investor and be put into practice, it would create more jobs, which in turn would improve the country's infrastructure.

Website design

INTRO

The Homepage

This is the prototype of how the frontpage will look like. Here you can access information about the project, creators and view photos of their work. On the right-top there is a log in button. It is for both investors and ideators to create a user and fullfil their reason of visit. (momina)

Search engine

After logging in, this is what the page will look like for the investors. Here they will be able to look after ideas and support the ladies. They can either search for a specific idea or choose from the categories listed. We have added a location category. With this it will be easier them to find ideas from their preferable geographical location. It will also help them to narrow down the options available.

Presentation of the Ideas

On the picture above, you can see an example of how the ideas are presented. In the left-hand side is a picture added by the ideator with a short description of the idea on the right. If the investors are interested, they can book an appointment with the ideator. We have decided to not showcase name and picture of the ideators. It is to prevent investors choosing people over ideas. The investor will only get contact information after booking a meeting.
